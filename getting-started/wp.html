Documentation

Windows Phone

System Requirements

Windows Phone 7.5 and higher are supported.

Installation

Install the MusicPimp app to your Windows Phone device.

Usage

  1. Add music endpoints to your app. Any PC on which MusicPimp is installed works as a music endpoint.
  2. Set the PC as the music source or use it for music playback in the app.

HTTPS

You may wish to connect to your music server over HTTPS. HTTPS is currently supported on Windows Phone provided that the server certificate passes validation and is trusted by your phone. This means that self-signed certificates will most likely not work. Commercial certificates are likely to work.

Miscellaneous

MusicPimp for Windows Phone also supports the Subsonic media streamer as a music server.
